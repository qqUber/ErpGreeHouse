## Tasks

- [ ] Define H1-H6 hierarchy in theme.ts

- [ ] Ensure proper font weights

### Task 2: Color Palette
- [ ] Define primary, secondary, accent colors
- [ ] Add neutral background shades

- [ ] Include CTA buttons
